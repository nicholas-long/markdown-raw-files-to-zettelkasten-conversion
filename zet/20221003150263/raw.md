### ECB CBC CFB OFB CTR cipher modes
- modes of operation
  - ECB
  - CBC
  - CFB
  - OFB
  - CTR
- Notable block ciphers
  - Lucifer / DES
  - IDEA
  - RC5
  - Rijndael / AES
  - Blowfish
